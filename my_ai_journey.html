AI Tools I'm Learning & Using

ChatGPT

ChatGPT by OpenAI is my go-to conversational AI for brainstorming, writing, and problem-solving. It excels at understanding context and maintaining engaging conversations. I use it for everything from coding help to creative writing and research assistance. Its ability to explain complex concepts in simple terms makes it perfect for learning new subjects.

What makes ChatGPT unique:
  • Excellent conversational flow and context retention
  • Strong creative writing and storytelling capabilities
  • Great for educational explanations and tutoring
  • Web browsing and image generation (GPT-4)
Claude AI

Claude AI (that's me!) is my companion for thoughtful analysis and detailed work. I'm particularly good at handling long documents, complex reasoning, and providing balanced perspectives. Students love using me for research, essay writing, and breaking down complicated topics into digestible insights with proper citations and structured thinking.

What makes Claude special:
  • Superior document analysis and long-form content handling
  • Strong ethical reasoning and balanced perspectives
  • Excellent at structured thinking and detailed explanations
  • Real-time web search and interactive artifacts
Google Gemini

Google Gemini is my research powerhouse, seamlessly integrated with Google's ecosystem. It excels at finding current information, analyzing data, and connecting insights across multiple sources. I use it when I need the latest information or want to leverage Google's vast knowledge base for academic projects and fact-checking.

Gemini's strengths:
  • Deep integration with Google services and real-time data
  • Excellent for research and fact-finding
  • Strong multimodal capabilities (text, image, video)
  • Great for academic and scientific queries
GitHub Copilot

GitHub Copilot is my coding companion that transforms how I write and learn programming. It suggests code completions, helps debug errors, and teaches me best practices through intelligent suggestions. As a student learning to code, it's like having a senior developer mentoring me 24/7, making complex programming concepts more accessible.

Why Copilot stands out:
  • Specialized for coding and software development
  • Context-aware code suggestions and completions
  • Integrated directly into development environments
  • Trained on billions of lines of code from GitHub
Gamma

Gamma revolutionizes how I create presentations and documents. Instead of spending hours on design, I simply input my content and Gamma creates beautiful, professional presentations automatically. It's perfect for academic presentations, project pitches, and creating visually appealing documents that engage audiences without the design learning curve.

Gamma's unique features:
  • AI-powered presentation and document creation
  • Automatic design and layout optimization
  • Interactive and web-native presentations
  • No design skills required for professional results
Midjourney

Midjourney unleashes my creative side through AI-generated artwork and visual concepts. I use it to create stunning visuals for projects, explore artistic ideas, and bring abstract concepts to life. It's incredible how I can describe an idea in words and receive professional-quality artwork that enhances my presentations and creative projects.

Midjourney's artistic edge:
  • Specialized in high-quality artistic image generation
  • Exceptional at creative and abstract concepts
  • Strong community and collaborative features
  • Unique artistic styles and aesthetic control
